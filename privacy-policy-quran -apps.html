Privacy Policy for Quran Al Barkah

Effective Date: May 17, 2025

Quran Al Barkah ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Location Information

We request access to your device’s location to:

  • Determine Qibla direction accurately.
  • Display nearby mosques.
  • Calculate prayer times based on your current location.

This data is used only within the app and is not stored or shared with any third party.

b. Non-Personal Usage Data

We may collect anonymous data such as:

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • General usage statistics

This data is used solely to improve the app’s performance and stability.

c. Audio Features

When you play Quran recitations, the audio is streamed or downloaded locally. The app does not access your microphone or record any voice data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.
  • Show relevant nearby places (e.g., mosques).
  • Improve app performance and fix issues.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sharing with third parties.

3. What We Don’t Do

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or personal identifiers.
  • We do not store your location permanently.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or third parties.

4. Permissions We Request

Permission Purpose
ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Qibla direction, nearby mosques, prayer times
INTERNET Audio streaming, content updates
FOREGROUND_SERVICE (if used) Background Quran audio playback

5. Children’s Privacy

Quran Al Barkah is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please use this app with parental guidance.

6. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal information. Location data is used temporarily while the app is active and is not stored on our servers.

7. Third-Party Services

Some services (e.g., Google Maps API or Firebase Analytics) may be used in the app. These services have their own privacy policies and terms. We encourage users to review them individually.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our official website at:

https://gridiyans.my.id/privacy-policy
